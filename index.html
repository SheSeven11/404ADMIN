Our Story

Built in the Streets. Proven Behind Bars. Carried Forward.

YOU-TH ARE THE FUTURE was not built in a boardroom. It was built in the streets, the cells, the classrooms, and the corners of Atlanta — by a man who knows every one of those places firsthand.

The founder grew up in the very communities this organization serves. In 1989, a series of choices led to a life sentence. He served 25 years. Inside those walls, the mission was being born.

"He became the first inmate in the Georgia Prison System to create and operate a youth mentorship program — transferred to three different prisons specifically to implement it."

Upon his release in 2013, he didn't walk away from the work — he walked further into it. Mentorship classes across the Georgia school system. Street-level violence interruption. Real change, with data to prove it. Throughout Atlanta he is known as "UNK," "OG," and "Mr. So ATL" — a name given to him in 1991 that has never stopped fitting.

YOU-TH ARE THE FUTURE is not just an organization. It is the life's work of a man who refused to let his worst chapter be his final one — and who has spent every day since proving that.

Our Next Chapter

Da Lab

Da Lab is our community wellness space — currently in development. A place where youth, families, and community members come to release, reset, and reconnect through creative therapy-based programming.

Animal-assisted interaction, expressive arts, movement, and mindfulness — all offered in a safe, judgment-free environment built by and for this community.

Da Lab offers creative therapy-inspired programming and is not a licensed clinical or therapeutic facility.
